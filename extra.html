Our equipment and floorplan

Our floorplan has been specifically chosen to fit our specific needs. The removal of anything non-essential makes it both less cluttered and more affordable for upkeep costs, making it better for our crew, consumers, and finance department. We have chosen highly regarded and very popular products for our plan, including a cooktop, deep fryer, fridge and sink, along with lots of counter space and a best-in-the-industry 6' fire suppressant system that doubles as a vent for ventilation. It's all powered by an industry-leading 5600W generator that keeps things running smoothly even when power is down everywhere. And our bulk-purchased silverware and plates allow our business to efficiently run for less, as well as some of the best knives for internal use and a great cutting board. And by shopping for multiple deals, our total pricing for our starting appliances and silverware is 7,051.55.

We also have a contactless-enabled printer, which enables us to take NFC payments, along with cash and standard cards.
